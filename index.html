Founder's Story: From Passion to Purpose
Dietitian Samawat — Clinical Dietitian • Wellness Coach • Research Scholar • Entrepreneur

NutriZerno began with a simple but powerful realization:

"I'm Dietitian Samawat, and as a dietitian I was guiding clients daily, helping them lose weight, balance hormones, and improve their health. But one challenge kept repeating."

NutriZerno was born from that insight: a need for healthy, trustworthy and convenient nutrition that truly supports modern wellness goals.

Dietician understood that many people were seeking effective solutions for weight loss, hormone balance, digestion and sustained energy, but the market offered either processed products, empty promises or complicated routines.

That led her to create NutriZerno — a premium wellness brand that blends scientific nutrition with traditional superfoods, starting with a modern Talbeena formulation crafted for everyday use.

Today, NutriZerno delivers premium nuts, seeds, Talbeena wellness blends and weight loss premixes that are personally formulated, tested and recommended based on real client needs.

Our brand promise is simple: natural ingredients, clean formulas and products made with the same care she would choose for her own clients and family.

Our Mission

To make healthy living simple, effective and accessible through functional nutrition rooted in real ingredients.

Our Vision

To become a trusted name in premium wellness nutrition for people seeking natural, result-driven support.
